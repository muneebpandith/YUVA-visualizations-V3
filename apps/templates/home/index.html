YUVA Datastack

A joint initiative by Mission YUVA, Department of Skill Development, Department of Labour & Employment of the Government of J&K, YUVA Datastack is a Digital Public Infrastructure aimed at research and development activities in advancing entrepreneurship opportunities, skill prospects, and workforce analytics for the youth of Jammu & Kashmir. This initiative seeks to foster data-driven policymaking and innovation, empowering young individuals with insights for better career and business prospects.

How everyone benefits from YUVA Datastack

Citizens

Helps individuals identify the best skill development programs and job opportunities based on their qualifications and industry trends. Provides data-driven insights on viable business opportunities, funding options, and sectoral demands.

R&D Organizations

Helps researchers analyze employment trends, skill gaps, and entrepreneurship growth in different sectors. Supports the creation of AI-driven tools, predictive analytics, and policy simulations. Assists in evaluating the effectiveness of government interventions and private sector contributions.

Academic Institutes

Helps universities and training centers align courses with market demands and future job trends. Provides real-world data for academic studies on labor markets, entrepreneurship, and economic development. Enables career services to offer personalized guidance based on industry insights.

Government

Enables data-driven decision-making for employment policies, skilling programs, and entrepreneurship incentives. Helps in identifying underserved areas, sectors with high employment potential, and necessary interventions. Supports the integration of public services with skill and employment databases for better governance.
